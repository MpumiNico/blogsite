Interview Tips

Be prepared to answer common interview questions like "Tell me about yourself" or "Why should we hire you?" Practice your answers ahead of time.

Research the company's mission, values, and culture. Know their products and services so you can ask informed questions during the interview.

First impressions matter. Dress appropriately for the role and company culture. Even if the company has a casual dress code, it's better to be slightly overdressed than underdressed.
